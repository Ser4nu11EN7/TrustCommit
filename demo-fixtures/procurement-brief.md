# Autonomous Support Queue Procurement Brief

The creator agent needs to choose a hosted model provider for an autonomous support queue.

Constraints:
- Monthly budget ceiling: 1200 USDC
- Required uptime: 99.9%
- Audit log export must be available
- Data retention must be configurable to 30 days or less
- The provider must support webhook delivery for agent receipts

Selection rule:
- Choose the provider that satisfies all hard constraints
- If both satisfy the hard constraints, prefer the lower monthly cost
